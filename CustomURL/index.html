Custom URL scheme Demo

The Custom URL scheme plugin allows your app to be launched by its URL scheme. Any other app, website, email link, etc can now launch your app. Any path and parameters specified in the URL will be made available to a Javascript method in your app.

Important
Make sure you specify the URL_SCHEME parameter in the plugin settings.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android - App package.

How to test this baby

Assuming the URL scheme of your app is 'mycoolapp', you can launch your app by clicking a link like <a href="mycoolapp://">. Note that in most cases typing in the URL in the address bar of your browser will not work.

So you could create a little website with a link, but for a quick test you can simply email yourself a link and open the email on your device. By clicking the link you will launch this app and we'll show you the launch URL.

Alter the email draft as you see fit. Make sure you use the same URL scheme as the one you configured in the plugin settings.
